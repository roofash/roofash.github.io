Djibouti
Djibouti is a maritime country in the eastern region of Africa. It is bordered by Ethiopia to the west, Eritrea to the north, Somalia to the south and east of Djibouti is the Gulf of Aden and the Red Sea. To top it all off, Djibouti is home to 920,000 people which also makes it the smallest country in continential Africa by population and Djibouti recieved 114,000 tourists in the year of 2021.
