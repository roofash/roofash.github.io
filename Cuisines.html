Cuisines
List some traditional custom dishes with images.

Djiboutian dishes are a mixture of french, south asian, and yemeni cuisine but with middle eastern spices. A popular djiboutian dish prepared usually during Eid al-Adha is skoudehkaris. Skoudehkaris is made with rice and usually lamb with cardamom, cilantro and cumin. Skoudehkaris is the national dish of Djibouti.

Another dish is Marake Kaloune which is a fish filet stew made of fish filet, potatoes, onions, okra, eggplants, tomatoes, garlic, tamarind paste, salt, pepper, parsley, rice, and oil.
