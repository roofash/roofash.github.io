Transportation

The airport you can fly into if you want to go to Djibouti is the Djibouti-Ambouli international airport located in the suburb of Ambouli. The cost for a roundway trip to Djibouti from Canada for 2 adults and kids is 2734 CAD.





For 1 adult, the prices are Economy is 3659, business is 10284, and first class is somehow 21031





The transportation methods you can use in Djibouti are taxis/cars which are mostly used in the downtown region, you can use water taxis aka ferries to travel from Djibouti city to obock, or to Yemen. Lastly, you can use regular buses to get from city to city. There used to be a railway system, although that has ceased operations.





All sources have said that using a tool like Google Maps would be your best bet to find destinations. Because Djibouti is a fairly small country, you would have a mostly easy time finding where to go by asking locals.





The cost for a 8 km taxi ride in Djibouti is 53 Canadian dollars. A car rental in Djibouti for a day is around 170 CAD. Djiboutian bus prices are usually 60 Canadian dollars per hour. A boat from the city of Obock to Moulhoule (north to south) is 15 hours and takes 2 hours (90 kilometer distance).
