Attractions
3-4 fun activities.
Points of interest, sporting venues, museums, etc. Descriptions, prices, website link.



Mosquee Al-Hamoudi:
Mosque Website

Built in 1906, architect name is Hamoudi Ahmed, colors include green, yellow, brown and blue, of which 2 of those colors are present in the djibouti flag, can also accommodate 1000 people

Lac ‘Assal:
Lake Website

Something I found really cool is that Lac ‘Assal is the lowest elevation point in Africa at 157 meters below sea level. Lac ‘Assal is also 10x saltier than sea water which makes it the 3rd most salty body of water.

Moucha Island:
Moucha Islands Website

Island made of sand and fossilized coral, hundreds of tiny lizards live here, nearby water is good to scuba dive in, there are many sharp bits on the floor so you better have good shoes

Ardoukoba:
Ardoukoba Website

Last eruption was back in 1978, the summit/highest point of the volcano is 298 m high. 3000 people live within 1km of this volcano. Rock types are Basalt / Picro-Basalt, Trachybasalt / Tephrite Basanite and Rhyolite.
